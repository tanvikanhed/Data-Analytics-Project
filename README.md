# 📊 Customer Churn Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
Customer churn occurs when a customer stops using a company’s service, leading to revenue loss.  
Analyzing churn helps businesses understand why customers leave and how to improve retention.  
High churn rates can affect revenue and business growth. By analyzing churn patterns, businesses can take proactive steps to retain customers.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Identify key factors affecting customer churn  
- Perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)  
- Visualize churn patterns using Power BI  
- Generate insights to improve customer retention  

---

## 📂 Dataset Information
The dataset contains customer-related information such as:

- **Demographics:** Gender, Senior Citizen  
- **Account Info:** Tenure, Contract Type  
- **Services:** Internet Service, Phone Service  
- **Billing:** Monthly Charges, Total Charges  
- **Target Variable:**  
  - `0` → Customer Stayed  
  - `1` → Customer Churned  

---

---

## 🔍 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Analyzed churn distribution  
- Studied relationship between tenure and churn  
- Compared monthly charges with churn  
- Examined service-based churn patterns  

---

## 📊 Dashboard (Power BI)
The Power BI dashboard includes:
- Churn Rate KPI  
- Customer Segmentation  
- Service-wise Churn Analysis  
- Interactive Filters for better insights  

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Customers with **higher monthly charges** are more likely to churn  
- Customers with **short tenure** show higher churn rate  
- **Month-to-month contracts** have higher churn  
- Some services (like fiber optic) show increased churn  

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Offer discounts to high-risk customers  
- Encourage long-term contracts  
- Improve service quality  
- Provide loyalty and engagement programs
